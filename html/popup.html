Submission confirmation

This is not for requesting a theme. I don't create themes by request.

Your submission will include your current thematic settings, such as your card images, dark mode, and font.

Make sure your theme follows these guidelines:

  • Appropriate images/title
  • Does not heavily use components from other themes
  • Uses a unique custom dark mode (not one of the presets)
  • Custom images cannot be from Discord or Gmail. These images are not public
